About This Application

The Hostel Room Finder is designed to help students and guests easily locate their rooms in the hostel complex. Features include:

  • Search for rooms by building, floor, and room type
  • Interactive floor maps to visualize room locations
  • Room availability information
  • Directions to your room from common entry points

For any issues or to report bugs, please contact the hostel administration office.
